Murang’a University of Technology Innovation and Industrial Linkages Week

About Innovation Week

1st Murang’a University of Technology Innovation Week!

THEME

ADVANCING CUTTING EDGE INNOVATIONS THROUGH SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY FOR SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT

 

Join us for the inaugural MUT Innovation Week, a week-long celebration of creativity, technology, and collaboration, taking place from 21st – 23rd November-2024 at Main Campus

SPONSORS

Explore Our Partiners
  • Sustainability
  • Digital Transformation
  • Inclusive Innovation
  • Sustainable Engineering
Explore our Items

What to Expect:

Exhibitions:

Discover groundbreaking projects and inventions by our talented students and faculty.

Keynote Addresses:

Engage with industry experts and thought leaders as they share insights on the latest trends in engineering, technology, and innovation.

Networking Opportunities:

Connect with fellow innovators, entrepreneurs, and professionals from various fields.

Keynote Speakers:-

Join us as we host renowned speakers who will share insights on the future of innovation and technology in Kenya.

Competition With Prizes:-

Participate in exciting challenges that showcase your skills and creativity for a chance to win fantastic prizes.
